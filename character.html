Character Preview

When creating a custom character in Goldsrc, there is a preview image shown in the menu to allow you to see what your character will look like. These images are produced using the same method as the character textures, but getting your hands on the remapped texture is much easier. In fact, Goldsrc will do it for you. After you have saved your character, exit the game and navigate to your game install folder. Depending on the game, you will then need to go into a game specific folder. Half-Life is /valve, Opposing Force is /gearbox, Team Fortress is /tfc, Deathmatch Classic is /dmc, and so on. Then go to models/player, remapped.bmp is the file you’re looking for.

You also can find your colored spray in your game folder, under /logos. The spray will be named remapped.bmp.
